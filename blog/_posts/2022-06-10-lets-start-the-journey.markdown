So, this is the first post on my new blog, but **Zero Signal** will be less of a typical blog and more a periodical, "quick checking-in" journal of my everyday life.

<!--more--> 

I've had a few of blogs in the past dealing with different topics, but after a while I've lost interest in all of them and they were short-lived, so hopefully this one will get a bit of traction and I'll update it more regularly.

I will probably mostly write about the movies I've seen, the places I've traveled or hiked to, a bit about the demoscene, and I'll possibly have a few posts about programming, graphics, and/or just random posts about nothing :)

Some technical background: I was thinking a lot if I should build up a blog engine on my own, or use a ready-made one, but in the end, in order to keep the site hosted on GitHub Pages, I've decided beside a static site generator, [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/). It's absolutely awesome so far :)

Oh, and why Zero Signal? It's one of my [favorite songs from Fear Factory](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HNvqcwIV8B0) :)
